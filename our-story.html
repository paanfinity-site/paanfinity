Our Story

How a family ritual turned into a bite you can carry anywhere.

Paan has always been more than a snack — it's a ritual. The kind that closes out a meal, a conversation, a celebration. We grew up watching it folded by hand, leaf by leaf, and wanted to find a way to carry that feeling with us, wherever we went.

Why "Bites"?

Traditional paan takes time, ingredients, and a steady hand. We wanted something that kept the spirit — the sweet paan masala, the lingering freshness — but fit in a pocket, a bag, a glovebox. So we built the Sweet Paan Bite: handcrafted in small batches, no leaf-folding required.

Handcrafted, on Purpose

We make our bites in small batches rather than mass-producing them, because the texture and flavor balance is easy to lose at scale. It takes longer, but it's the difference between a bite that tastes like an afterthought and one that tastes like tradition.
