Returns

Because Paanfinity is a food product, our return policy works a little differently.

Food Safety First

For health and safety reasons, we're unable to accept returns of opened food items.

Damaged or Incorrect Orders

If your order arrives damaged, or you received the wrong item, contact us within 7 days of delivery with a photo, and we'll make it right with a replacement or refund.

Unopened Items

Unopened, unexpired items can be returned within 14 days of delivery for a refund, minus original shipping costs.
